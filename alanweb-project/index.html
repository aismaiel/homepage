About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Pennsylvania, with an expected graduation date of May 2022. Working towards a Bachelors of Science in Engineering (B.S.E.) in Computer and Information Sciences, as well as minors in Statistics, Math, and English.

In addition to my studies, I work at the university's PRECISE Center programming and researching tools to generate multivariate time series data from probabilistic and independence specifications. Currently, I am also the Head Teaching Assistant for Automata, Computability, and Complexity (CIS 262), Penn's course exploring the theory of computation for undergraduate students.

Outside of this, I also help organize the Science Olympiad at Penn high school tournament, volunteer at Moelis Access Science to teach CS to high school students in Philadelphia, and proudly play saxophone in the Penn Band. In my free time, I like to play all kinds of video games, and watch low quality television shows wherever they are available. Currently looking for people to watch "The Bachelor" with.
